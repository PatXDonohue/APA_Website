About the APA

The Abington Pickleball Association (APA) is a community organization for players aged 50 and older. We maintain courts, organize play, and host tournaments and social events throughout the season.

Policies & Procedures

Download Policies (PDF)

Membership

Members must be 50 years of age or older. Annual membership runs from April 1 through March 30. Non-resident membership is limited; if the cap is reached, applicants will be added to a waitlist.

Court Use

Courts are open to members during posted hours. Please observe court etiquette, respect rotation, and yield to organized play when scheduled.

Guests

Guests must be at least 50 years old, must sign the release waiver, and must pay the guest fee before playing. Members are limited in how often they may bring the same guest in a season — check with the board for details.

Conduct

We expect courteous, respectful behavior at all times. Anyone violating the code of conduct may have their membership reviewed by the board.

Challenge Play

Members who meet the skill criteria may participate in Challenge Play. Contact a board member to be evaluated.
